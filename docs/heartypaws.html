HEARTY PAWS A pet care app thoughtfully crafted for busy pet owners.
ROLE UX Designer.
TIMELINE Oct 2024 – Feb 2025.

Description

This project was created to address the need for a pet care platform that caters to a variety of pet owners. There are a few pet care platforms in the market that offer services for new pet users and owners with multiple pets. According the the conducted research, most users feel overwhelmed by the complexity of the current available pet care platforms.

The idea was to create a pet care plat form that provided customized care for pet owners while also having an option to book vet and grooming appointments from the same platform.

The Pitch

The platform will feature personalized care reminders, exercise tracking and also allow users to book veterinary appointments and grooming sessions right from the app.

Problem Overview
What's the background of the project
HeartyPaws is a pet care platform designed to simplify pet care experience
It provides pet owners with digital tool to track their pet's activities
The platform will feature personalized care reminders and also allow users to book vet appointments
Research Goals
What do we want to achieve during Research Phase?
We want to understand the challenges pet owners face in managing pet care routines
Identify the key features users would expect in a pet care app
Deternine what would make users consistently use Hearty Paws and its features

The Process

The Design started with user research for different pet owners : new pet owners, busy pet owners and some multi-pet owners. The process started out with a bit of background research into the pain points of pet owners using other pet care apps.

After defining key objectives , around 5 user interviews were performed on various kinds of pet owners. This help draw some insight on what features are missing from the current competition.

The Design

A rough sketch was created to define the structure of the application. The sketch defined the Happy Path of a user right from installing the app to booking their first vet visit for their pet..

01. Login
The Users can use their login information and Log -on to the app. The users can use their email or they can use their social media accounts to signup if they haven’t already
02. Pet Profile
The Users make the profile of their pets adding details like name, health medical history, breed etc.
03. Vet Search
The Users can search and decide between various vets to see who fits them. Filers like location proximity and other user ratings
04. Vet Profile
The Users can view each vet’s profile, read descriptions and see contact details and times the vet are available. users can also book appoints
05. Date Selection
After clicking on “Book Appointments” a pop up allows the users to select the desired date for the appointment. There's is always an option to collapse the Popup to go back
06. Time Selection
After confirming the Date the users can select the time slot and provide a reason for the appointment There's is always an option to go back

The final draft was refined according to user feedback and a orange - green color pallet was applied. The design also features a dashboard with th ability to add multiple pets (which was missing in the rough draft).

The vet section was also re-designed to include a messaging feature to directly send texts to vets. Also a paws system was added to act as rating for the vets...

The Outcome

The design of the pet care app “Hearty paws” helped me learn the entire process of user experience, right from the user research and initial data gathering to the refining of teh UI Elements according to feedback.

Overall , building the app also helped me understand the true potential of Figma for designing UI experiences.
